God’s Love Never Fails

No matter what you've done, God’s love remains. His mercy is new every morning, and His grace reaches farther than our failures. The Bible says in Romans 8:38-39 that nothing can separate us from the love of God in Christ Jesus.

Let this be your reminder today: you are deeply loved by the Creator of the universe. Run to Him, rest in Him, and walk in His unfailing love.

“The steadfast love of the Lord never ceases; his mercies never come to an end; they are new every morning.” – Lamentations 3:22-23
