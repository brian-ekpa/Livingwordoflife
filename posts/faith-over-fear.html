Faith Over Fear

Fear is real, but faith is stronger. God has not given us a spirit of fear, but of power, love, and a sound mind (2 Timothy 1:7). When we choose faith, we choose to trust God’s promises over our problems.

Even when life feels shaky, God remains unshaken. Let His Word be your anchor and His peace guide your heart.

“When I am afraid, I put my trust in you.” – Psalm 56:3
